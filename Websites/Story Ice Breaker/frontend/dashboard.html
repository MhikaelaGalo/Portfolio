THE PROBLEM
Why a Safer Platform is Needed

Most domestic worker hirings in the Philippines happen through informal channels — Facebook posts, group chats, referrals, and unregistered agents. This puts everyone at risk.

Facebook & Group Chat Hiring

Hiring through social media and informal chats offers no protection or accountability.

Scams & Fake Identities

Without verification, both workers and employers risk fraud and deception.

Unclear Job Agreements

Informal hiring leads to misunderstandings about pay, duties, and schedules.

Unsafe Work Arrangements

Workers often face unregulated conditions with no channels for reporting abuse.
