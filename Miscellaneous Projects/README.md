# Systems and Business Development Portfolio

This repository features a collection of professional projects ranging from digital platform solutions to hospitality and food service ventures. Each project applies strategic frameworks like Design Thinking, PESTEL analysis, and detailed financial forecasting to solve specific market needs within the Philippine context.

## Project Directory

### 1. MetroStay: Dorm and Apartment Finder Solution
MetroStay is a digital platform and mobile application designed to address leasing challenges in Metro Manila by connecting students and young professionals with verified housing.

* **Problem Statement**: Tackles the "leasing woes" of Metro Manila, including unverified listings and fragmented information for affordable housing.
* **Key Features**: Verified property listings, map-based search, and integrated digital payments.
* **Tech Stack**: Google Firebase, GCash/PayMaya API integration, and an Android (.apk) demo.
* **Business Analysis**: Includes a full Business Model Canvas, PESTEL analysis, and a 3-year financial projection.

### 2. Good Vibes Travel and Tours (GV) Website Proposal
A technical proposal focused on digital transformation for a travel and tour agency to enhance its online presence and service accessibility.

* **Problem Statement**: Addresses the limitations of an outdated web presence that resulted in high bounce rates and low customer engagement.
* **Proposed Solution**: A new official website developed using the Design Thinking process to showcase unique, off-the-beaten-path travel packages.
* **Key Focus**: Improving user navigation, streamlining the booking process, and highlighting diverse services like team-building and charity activities.
* **Analysis**: Features feasibility and impact studies, client interview insights, and draft website layouts.

### 3. Arroz Por Favor: Filipino-Mexican Fusion
A business pitch for a student-focused food brand that combines Mexican seasonings with Filipino rice culture.

* **Vision**: To become a beloved fusion brand by serving flavorful, affordable, and "student-friendly" meals.
* **Unique Selling Point**: Offers bold Mexican flavors in a convenient, easy-to-carry rice box tailored for busy campus schedules.
* **Menu Highlights**: Tacos de Pollo Crujiente, Tex-Mex Beef, and specialty drinks like Agua Fresca de Pina Colada.
* **Financials**: Detailed breakdown of ingredient costs, profit margins (averaging 32%–39%), and daily revenue projections.
