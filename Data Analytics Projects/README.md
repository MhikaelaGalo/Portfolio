### 2. Heart Attack Risk Prediction
* [cite_start]**Purpose**: Uses statistical modeling to predict the likelihood of heart attacks based on patient biomarkers and vital signs[cite: 601, 606].
* [cite_start]**Methodology**: Employs **Binomial Logistic Regression** to model the log-odds of a positive diagnosis[cite: 601, 602].
* [cite_start]**Key Predictors**: Analyzes variables such as age, gender, heart rate, blood pressure, blood sugar, CK-MB, and troponin[cite: 603, 608].
* [cite_start]**Insights**: Identifies high-risk patients to support clinical decision-making and early screening[cite: 614].

### 3. MPG Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* **Purpose**: An exploratory analysis of vehicle fuel efficiency (Miles Per Gallon) using Python.
* **Tools**: Python libraries including **Pandas**, **NumPy**, **Matplotlib**, and **Seaborn**.
* **Key Tasks**:
    * Data cleaning and distribution analysis (e.g., weight, acceleration, and horsepower).
    * Visualizing relationships between car attributes and their origin (USA, Japan, Europe).
    * Statistical summaries including mean, median, skewness, and kurtosis of MPG data.
